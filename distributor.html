Welcome to Torero Corporation Pvt. Ltd.

BRAND OWNING GLOBAL FASHION HOUSE

Torero is among the highest quality leather fashion accessory manufacturers in the world. Torero is the exclusive Global Licensee for powerful global brands such as CROSS, POLICE and SWISS MILITARY. A wide array of products ranging from handcrafted premium leather wallets to exquisitely designed bags are offered. Torero's products are currently available in more than 50 countries across Asia, Europe and the America through Retail, Corporate Gift, E-commerce, In-flight channels, etc. Torero’s expertise and State of the Art manufacturing facilities in Kolkata makes it a one stop hub for small leather accessories/goods requirements. Torero’s expertise lies in Intellectual Property Rights, Design, Operational Excellence and Global Distribution Network. Torero also manufacturers for brands such as Mont Blanc, Hugo Boss, Cerruti 1881, Guy Laroche and more.

Torero is an extension of a premiere leather export company Metropoli Fashions Pvt. Ltd. which has been manufacturing the finest leather goods, in Kolkata, India since 1988.

Torero offers a wide array of products ranging from handcrafted premium leather wallets to exquisitely designed bags. Under the SLG category we offer men’s wallet, women’s wallet, belts, smart wallets, card holders, keychain, tag, tech case, phone case and desktop stationaries. Our LLG category comprises of men’s briefcase, crossbody, backpack, women’s bag and sling bag. We have recently added luggage in our product portfolio.
